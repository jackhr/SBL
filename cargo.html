Typhoon Express & Leeward Express

If you're planning on carrying large cargo, including vehicles, the Typhoon Express & Leeward Express are for you:

  • • Sails every Wednesday & Friday.
  • • Departs from Crabbs Marina in Antigua.
  • • Typhoon Express has a capacity for 39 passengers.
  • • Leeward Express has a capacity for 81 passengers.
  • • Large variety of cargo.
  • • Can carry vehicles, but not for return on the same day.
  • • Weather always plays a part in safety and we reserve the right to delay or cancel trips if needed
